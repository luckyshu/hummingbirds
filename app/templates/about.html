About the project

HummingBirds is incubated from InsightDataScience in 2013. This application helps people get the trending news efficiently based on trends in Twitter. When the phrase of trend is ambiguous, it is quite time consuming for people to get the idea. By gathering related information with Twitter API and Bit.ly API, news is listed with the related trend order by timestamp.

How to find related news

Links: links in tweets and Bit.ly search results

Trend phrase set: [tread words, most frequent words in tweets]

News info set: [title, meta info, first 150 words in article text]

Method: keyword searching

How to analyze sentiment of trend

Tweets: use Twitter API to search the related 100 tweets

NLTK method: use 100000+ pre-classified tweets as train set for classifier training and use 10000 as test set. False Negative: ~40%, False Postive: ~50%

Token method: use words with pre-computed sentiment score to calculate the sentiment score of tweets. False Negative: ~10%, False Postive: ~20%
